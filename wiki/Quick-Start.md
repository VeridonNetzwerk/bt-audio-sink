# Quick Start

Go from zero to Bluetooth audio streaming in a few minutes.

## 1. Install the app

- Install BT Audio Sink with the MSI package or extract the portable build.
- Start the app.

## 2. Pair your Bluetooth device

- Open Windows Settings -> Bluetooth & devices.
- Pair your phone with the PC.

## 3. Connect

- Open BT Audio Sink.
- Select your device in the list.
- Click **Connect**.

## 4. Start music playback

- Start audio on your phone.
- The sound will play through your PC.

## Done

Your PC is now working as a Bluetooth audio sink.
